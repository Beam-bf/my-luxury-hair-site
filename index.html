Premium Hair Care Instructions

Treat your extensions with love. Follow our luxury routine to keep your human hair & blends beautifully lustrous for years.

01

Washing Ritual

Always comb gently using a wide-toothed comb before washing. Wash thoroughly with sulfate-free shampoo in cool or lukewarm water. For synthetic blends, avoid vigorous rubbing and air dry.

02

Hydrate & Protect

Condition natural hair once a week with argan or macadamia oils. For synthetic blends, use specialized leave-in detangling sprays. Always apply a heat protectant spray prior to styling.

03

Nightly Sleep Routine

Gently tie hair or loosely braid before sleeping. Wrap your head elegantly in a satin or silk bonnet, or sleep directly on high-quality luxury silk pillowcases to prevent dry friction.
